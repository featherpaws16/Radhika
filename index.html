Mission Statement: Research at the Advanced Materials Processing Research (AMP) Lab at Virginia Commonwealth University is both fundamental and applied in character. Our overarching mission is to identify and exploit fundamental connections between crystal structure, microstructure and functional response in select magnetic materials systems, with the goal to enable advanced applications in the power, energy and biomedical sector.
